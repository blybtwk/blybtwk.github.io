Altinea is a modern serif typeface that redefines typographic proportions, breaking traditional conventions with elegance and precision. At its core lies the reshaping of height dynamics, where the x-height is compressed to half its traditional size, while the ascenders and descenders are dramatically elongated to create striking vertical contrast and visual rhythm.
Altinea compresses the x-height, giving the typeface a light, airy quality. Meanwhile, the towering ascenders and deep descenders stretch the boundaries of form, creating a sense of structure and vertical grace.
